Ajay Jadhav

I am a technology entrepreneur, working as Co-founder & CTO at AyanWorks Technology Solutions (out of Pune, India) on Self-Sovereign Identity and Decentralized Identity systems. CREDEBL - Decentralized Identity Platform

LF Decentralized Trust Project | Digital Public Good

Population-scale platform for Decentralized Identity and Verifiable Credentials management. Multi-tenant, agent-agnostic, and ledger-agnostic platform providing end-to-end verifiable credential lifecycle management. Successfully deployed for National Digital Identity in Bhutan (NDI) and Papua New Guinea (SevisPass Digital ID).
